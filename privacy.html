Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how ClientFlow ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our client management and messaging platform.

1. Information we collect

  • Account information — names, email addresses, and login credentials of users who manage a workspace.
  • Client data — contact details and records that our customers store about their own clients in order to manage and communicate with them.
  • Communications — messages sent and received through WhatsApp, SMS and email, including delivery and read status.
  • Usage data — technical logs such as IP address, browser type, and activity needed to operate and secure the service.

2. How we use information

  • To provide client management, reminders, and messaging features.
  • To send and receive messages on behalf of our customers over WhatsApp, SMS and email.
  • To maintain the security, reliability, and performance of the service.
  • To provide customer support and respond to inquiries.

3. WhatsApp Business messaging

ClientFlow uses the official WhatsApp Business Platform to send and receive messages. Messages are processed solely to deliver the communications our customers initiate or that their clients send to them. We do not sell message content, and we do not use it for advertising. Use of WhatsApp is also governed by WhatsApp's and Meta's own terms and policies.

4. Data sharing

We do not sell personal data. We share data only with service providers that help us operate the platform (such as cloud hosting, messaging, and email delivery providers), and only as needed to provide the service or as required by law.

5. Data retention

We retain data for as long as a customer's account is active or as needed to provide the service, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements.

6. Security

We use industry-standard measures to protect data, including encryption of sensitive integration credentials and secure transport (HTTPS). No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, but we work to protect your information.

7. Your rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. To make a request, contact us using the details below.
