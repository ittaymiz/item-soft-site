About

ClientFlow is a client management and communication platform operated by Ittay, based in Petah Tikva, Israel. It is built for care and service organizations: each team manages its own clients, documents and messaging integrations in a secure, multi-workspace environment. We help organizations stay on top of client follow-ups and communicate reliably across WhatsApp, SMS and email.
